Sales CRM

Lead scoring, follow-ups, interaction history and a KPI dashboard on top of Odoo CRM.

Sales CRM extends Odoo's native CRM with automated lead scoring, scheduled follow-up reminders, a customer interaction log, security-scoped pipelines, printable QWeb reports and an OWL-powered KPI dashboard.

  • Automated lead scoring from budget, engagement and recency
  • Scheduled follow-ups with an overdue reminder cron
  • Full customer interaction history (calls, emails, meetings)
  • Salesperson / Manager / Admin security groups and record rules
  • Pivot & graph analytics plus a QWeb PDF pipeline report
  • Custom OWL dashboard with live pipeline KPIs
